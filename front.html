JanuSequence

JanuSequence is an online recreational game created by Group 2 of Grade 12 TVL-Programming A students for their COR 020 (Physical Education) exam in the academic year 2024–2025. Tasked with developing their own recreational game, they brought their programming skills and creativity to life by building a unique and challenging web-based game.

How to Play JanuSequence:
1. Watch the sequence of colors flash.
2.Memorize the pattern in order.
3. Repeat the pattern in reverse
by tapping the correct colors.
4.Keep up — the sequence gets longer each
round. One mistake, and it’s game over!
